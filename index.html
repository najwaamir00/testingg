About Me

Born and raised in Ipoh, I am a 20 years-old final year student at University Teknologi Mara, Kedah, majoring in information management. For my degree, I am interested in studying information system. I am very interested in my coding class and decided to continue in information system. Coding has always been an interesting subject for me, however, I never had the experience before to thoroughly understand the function and how to code. I also interested in designing, I already know how to use adobe and wanting to learn more new stuffs. I am open to any new subject and would like to try it first, for me to have an opinion on it. I am also interested in writing, especially creative writing such as poem and novels. I have my own blog that focuses on K-pop, however, that website has not been active due to me having to focus on my study. On my free time, I like to listen to songs. I have many playlists that have different purposes. One for studying, one for relaxing before sleeps and another for exercising, and many more. Admittedly, my playlist consists of a good amount of K-pop, with a pinch of heavy metal, pop-punk and western song.
